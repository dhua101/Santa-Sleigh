Christmas

Christmas is an annual holiday to celebrate the birth of Jesus Christ. The ananal festival is celebrated and held on December 25. Christmastide is the season in the West that last for 12 days. Twilfth Night with some traditions of Christmastide includes Octave.

The holiday season for Christmas consist of 2 days: Christmas Eve on December 24 and Christmas Day on December 25. Those 2 days has the main focus of gift-giving and sharing traditional meal with family.

There are plenty of activities and festivities for the people to be doing while Christmas season is going around.

Activities and Festivities
  • Gift giving
  • Completing Advent Calendar
  • Completing Advent Wreath
  • Christmas Decorations
  • Exchanging Christmas cards
  • Christmas music
  • Singing Christmas carols
  • Lighting Christingle
  • Special meal
  • Church services

There are interchangeable figures that are shown at Christmas time. Santa Claus is the notable one. Other figures for the interchangeable in terms of gift giving in Christmas are Father Christmas, Saint Nicholas, and Christkind. The idea behind those figures is to deliver and please the children with presents and gifts.
